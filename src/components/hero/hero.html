Elon Musk

Administrator of the Department of Government Efficiency

Elon Musk, born June 28, 1971, is a businessman known for leading Tesla, SpaceX, and owning X Corp. (formerly Twitter). In January 2025, he became Administrator of the Department of Government Efficiency (DOGE) under Donald Trump's administration. Musk, founder of Neuralink, xAI, and OpenAI, is the world's wealthiest individual, with a net worth of $427 billion as of January 2025.
